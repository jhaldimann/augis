About AUGIS

What is AUGIS?

AUGIS is the project of our bachelor thesis and it stands for Autonomous Underwater Ground Image Stitcher. It is a project to create a small boat which can create Orthophotos of the seafloor.

Who is working on it?

We are two Computer Science students at the Bern University of Applied Sciences and we are currently in our last semester of our bachelor's degree program.
